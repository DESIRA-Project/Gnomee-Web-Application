About


DESIRA Gnomee offers an online knowledge base of Digital Tools that can act as potential Digital Game Changers in rural areas, agriculture and forestry. The inventory is initiated with around 700 Digital Tools that have been collected in the context of the DESIRA H2020 Research Project, by the project partners. These tools, are classified by a number of categories including the following:
  • Domain / Sub-domain / Application Scenario
  • Digital Technologies used by the Tool
  • Physical Digital Connection
  • Countries, in which the tool is being used

Gnomee allows its users to perform full text search and / or filter them based on any of the above classifications, thus enabling quick identification of tools of interest. It also supports browsing facility based on the above classifications and interactive visualisations of the available data.
